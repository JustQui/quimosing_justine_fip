About Us

Ca-paw-ble Felines, Fur Real!

Established in 2022, Kitty Kares aims to give opportunities to felines looking for a different purpose in life. We recognize that cats are capable of much more than being household companions. They can give so much more than companionship; their talents may benefit society as a whole.


By giving them a venue into the work field, we hope to also spread a message of workplace equality, regardless of species.
